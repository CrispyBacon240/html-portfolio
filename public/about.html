About Me

My life long dream has been to work in tech, to make a difference in people's lives. It all started when I was approximately 8 years old, curiosity got the best of me, at the time I disassembled a broken computer, tinkered around with spare parts from another computer and with some miracle, managed to get the broken computer working again, this sparked my curiosity for technology, a flame which has never been extinguished, thus I decided to enroll in the full stack web developer bootcamp to pursue my dream.

My end goal after completing the course is to build upon what I have learned, as well as to use what I have learned to build and implement applications that can improve quality of life for people as well as help solve real world challenges/problems, this is but the beginning of a long journey, I am confident after the completion of the course I will be in the position to pursue what I hope to achieve
